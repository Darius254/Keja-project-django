How to breach the gap between tenants Using Tech

As the number of students pursuing higher education continues to rise, ensuring convenient and accessible accommodation options becomes crucial. This project aims to explore innovative solutions for students and faculty members to easily access suitable housing near universities using a web app. After analyzing existing challenges, evaluating transportation networks, and leveraging technology, I can create a seamless experience for those transitioning to new environments. The project will focus on affordability, safety, proximity to campus, and sustainable practices.
